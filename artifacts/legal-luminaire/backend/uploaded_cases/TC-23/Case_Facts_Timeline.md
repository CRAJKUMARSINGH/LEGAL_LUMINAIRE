**Project:** Construction of 45 km Extension of NH-148B (Pali–Sumerpur Section), Rajasthan  
**Employer:** National Highways Authority of India (NHAI), PIU Jodhpur  
**Contractor:** M/s Roadcon Infra Pvt. Ltd., Jaipur  
**Contract Value:** ₹ 1,12,40,00,000 (One Hundred Twelve Crore Forty Lakh)  
**Contract Type:** EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction)

**Completion Period:** 30 months (by 28.02.2023)

## Key Legal Issues

1. **Wrongful termination** — Clause 16 (suspension for non-payment) vs. NHAI's Clause 62 (termination for default): Can NHAI terminate after contractor's valid suspension?  
2. **EOT for external delays** (utility, forest) — NHAI's obligation under GCC Clause 44  
3. **Price escalation** — Clause 10CA applicability, base date steel index  
4. **Three-member vs. sole arbitrator** — Clause 26 interpretation  
   - SC: Perkins Eastman Architects v. HSCC (2020) 20 SCC 760 — unilateral appointment invalid
