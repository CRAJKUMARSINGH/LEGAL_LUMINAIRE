## STRICT ACCURACY PROTOCOLS

**GUIDELINES VERSION**: 2.0  
**CREATION DATE**: April 3, 2026  
**STATUS**: DEMO / IMAGINARY / TEST DATA  

---

### **OVERVIEW**

These accuracy guidelines establish strict protocols for all Legal Luminaire content, ensuring 100% verification, no unverified claims, and complete transparency about synthetic data usage.

---

### **CORE ACCURACY PRINCIPLE**

**100% VERIFICATION REQUIRED** - No unverified claims, no assumptions, no invented content.

---

### **STRICT ACCURACY CHECKLIST**

#### **1. LEGAL CITATIONS**
- [ ] Every case law must have verified citation
- [ ] All statutory references must be accurate
- [ ] No fake or invented legal authorities
- [ ] All precedents must be traceable to real sources
- [ ] Citations must follow proper legal citation format

#### **2. FACTUAL ASSERTIONS**
- [ ] All facts must be verifiable
- [ ] No invented statistics or metrics
- [ ] All claims must have supporting evidence
- [ ] No speculation presented as fact
- [ ] All assumptions must be clearly labeled

#### **3. SYNTHETIC DATA HANDLING**
- [ ] All synthetic documents clearly labeled
- [ ] DEMO / IMAGINARY / TEST DATA labels required
- [ ] No real cases or persons represented
- [ ] Educational purpose disclosure required
- [ ] Professional disclaimer included

#### **4. PERFORMANCE CLAIMS**
- [ ] All performance metrics must be test-validated
- [ ] No exaggerated or unverified capabilities
- [ ] All benchmarks must be reproducible
- [ ] Clear disclosure of limitations required
- [ ] Conservative claim formulation

#### **5. MARKETING COMPLIANCE**
- [ ] All marketing claims must be evidence-based
- [ ] No misleading or exaggerated statements
- [ ] All success stories must be test-validated
- [ ] Clear disclosure of synthetic nature required
- [ ] Professional standards compliance
