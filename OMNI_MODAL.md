# OMNI-MODAL Protocol: Virtual Senior Advocate
**Omni-Modal Intelligence for Nascent Advocates (OMNI-MODAL)**

This document defines the core logic, workflow, and safety standards for the Legal Luminaire OMNI-MODAL engine.

---

## 🎯 Mission Statement
To empower **Nascent Advocates** (junior lawyers) by eliminating the manual data entry bottleneck. OMNI-MODAL transforms raw legal artifacts—smartphone photos of FIRs, scanned PDFs, or handwritten notes—into structured, courtroom-ready legal strategies and drafts.

---

## 🔄 Four-Phase Intelligence Workflow

### Phase 1: Ingestion & Foundation (The Dropzone)
- **Omni-Dropzone**: A unified entry point for all case artifacts (.PDF, .PNG, .JPG, .DOCX).
- **OCR/HTR Pipeline**: Automated text extraction from images and handwritten police records.
- **Persistent Instantiation**: Conversion of raw text into a permanent digital case file in the Legal Data Matrix.

### Phase 2: Structural Analysis (The Intelligence Layer)
- **Document Classification**: AI-driven categorization (e.g., FIR vs. Bail Order vs. Seizure Memo).
- **Fact Extraction**: Automatic identification of Accused Names, Statues (IPC/BNS), and Jurisdictions.
- **Timeline Heatmap**: Clustering of scattered dates into a chronological event sequence.
- **Tutorial Mode (Explanation Layer)**: Interactive tooltips that quote Supreme Court precedents to explain procedural defects to junior lawyers.

### Phase 3: Drafting & Research (The Action Layer)
- **Auto-Query Research**: Facts are instantly cross-referenced against the law database to fetch the Top 5 relevant precedents.
- **One-Click Drafting**: Instant generation of **§439 Bail** or **§250 Discharge** applications using extracted facts.
- **Safety Enforcement**: Mandatory citation verification scans to prevent AI hallucinations.
- **Court-Specific Styling**: Automatic application of formatting rules (e.g., Rajasthan High Court bolding vs. Supreme Court margins).

### Phase 4: Chamber Workflow (The Collaborative Layer)
- **Junior → Senior Review**: A multi-lawyer approval pipeline.
- **Human-in-the-Loop**: Split-screen verification where advocates approve AI extractions before final filing.
- **Regional Support**: Native support for Hindi/Devanagari script documents to ensure national accessibility.

---

## 🛡️ Safety & Integrity Standards
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Zero-Hallucination Mandate**: No document is permitted for export if it contains unverifiable legal citations.
> **Fact-Fit Grounding**: All strategy pillars must be linked to a specific page chunk in the original evidence.
