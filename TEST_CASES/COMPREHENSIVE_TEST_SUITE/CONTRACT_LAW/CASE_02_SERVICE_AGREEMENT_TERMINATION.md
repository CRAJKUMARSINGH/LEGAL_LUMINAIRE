# CONTRACT LAW TEST CASE 02: Service Agreement Termination

## DOCUMENT TEXT
```
MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement is entered into on 1st April, 2024

BETWEEN:

DataSecure Analytics Pvt. Ltd.
Registered Office: 789, Cyber Park, Hyderabad - 500001
(Hereinafter referred to as the "SERVICE PROVIDER")

AND

FinCorp Banking Solutions Ltd.
Registered Office: 321, Financial District, Chennai - 600001
(Hereinafter referred to as the "CLIENT")

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
The Service Provider shall provide comprehensive data analytics and cybersecurity services as detailed in Schedule B attached hereto.

2. CONTRACT PERIOD
This agreement shall be effective for a period of 3 years from the date of execution, i.e., from 1st April, 2024 to 31st March, 2027.

3. SERVICE FEES
Annual service fee: INR 1,20,00,000/-
Payable in quarterly installments of INR 30,00,000/- each.

4. TERMINATION CLAUSE
4.1 Either party may terminate this agreement by giving 90 days written notice.
4.2 The Client may terminate immediately if the Service Provider fails to maintain service levels as per SLA.
4.3 The Service Provider may terminate immediately if payments are delayed beyond 60 days.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties shall maintain strict confidentiality of all information exchanged during the term of this agreement.

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
All disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in Hyderabad as per Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

SIGNED:
For DataSecure Analytics Pvt. Ltd.
_________________________
CEO

For FinCorp Banking Solutions Ltd.
_________________________
CEO
```

## TERMINATION INCIDENT
- Client issued termination notice on 15th December, 2024 (effective 15th March, 2025)
- Reason: Alleged SLA breaches in Q3 2024
- Service Provider claims termination is wrongful
- Client withheld final quarter payment of INR 30,00,000/-
- Service Provider claims damages for wrongful termination

## LEGAL ISSUES TO ANALYZE
1. Whether termination notice complied with agreement terms?
2. Validity of SLA breach claims?
3. Entitlement to withheld payments?
4. Calculation of termination damages?
5. Arbitration proceedings strategy?
