# CONTRACT LAW TEST CASE 03: Joint Venture Dispute

## DOCUMENT TEXT
```
JOINT VENTURE AGREEMENT

This Joint Venture Agreement is made on 10th June, 2024

BETWEEN:

GreenTech Energy Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
Registered Office: 555, Eco Park, Pune - 411001
(Hereinafter referred to as "PARTY A")

AND

SolarPower International Ltd.
Registered Office: 777, Renewable Energy Zone, Ahmedabad - 380001
(Hereinafter referred to as "PARTY B")

WHEREAS both parties desire to form a joint venture company for the purpose of developing and operating solar power projects in India.

1. JOINT VENTURE COMPANY
Name: GreenSolar Ventures Pvt. Ltd.
Shareholding: Party A - 51%, Party B - 49%
Registered Office: Mumbai, Maharashtra

2. CAPITAL CONTRIBUTION
Total authorized capital: INR 50,00,00,000/-
Party A contribution: INR 25,50,00,000/-
Party B contribution: INR 24,50,00,000/-

3. MANAGEMENT
- Board of Directors: 7 members (Party A - 4, Party B - 3)
- Managing Director: To be appointed by Party A
- Key decisions require 75% board approval

4. PROFIT SHARING
Profits to be shared in proportion to shareholding ratios.

5. EXIT CLAUSE
Either party may exit after 3 years by offering shares to the other party first at fair market value.

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Disputes to be resolved through mediation followed by arbitration in Mumbai.

SIGNED:
For GreenTech Energy Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
_________________________
Managing Director

For SolarPower International Ltd.
_________________________
Managing Director
```

## DISPUTE INCIDENT
- After 18 months, Party B alleges mismanagement by Party A
- Party A accuses Party B of not providing promised technical expertise
- Joint venture company operating at loss
- Party B wants to exit; Party A refuses buyback at fair value
- Deadlock in board decisions

## LEGAL ISSUES TO ANALYZE
1. Validity of management allegations?
2. Breach of technical expertise obligations?
3. Exit clause interpretation and enforcement?
4. Fair market valuation methodology?
5. Deadlock resolution mechanisms?
