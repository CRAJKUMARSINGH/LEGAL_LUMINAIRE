# CONTRACT LAW TEST CASE 01: Sale Agreement Breach

## DOCUMENT TEXT
```
SALE AGREEMENT

This Sale Agreement is made and executed on this 15th day of January, 2025

BETWEEN:

M/s. TechVision Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
A company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013
Registered Office: 123, Tech Park, Bangalore - 560001
(Hereinafter referred to as the "SELLER")

AND

M/s. Global Retail Chain Ltd.
A company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013
Registered Office: 456, Mall Road, Mumbai - 400001
(Hereinafter referred to as the "BUYER")

1. SUBJECT MATTER
The Seller agrees to sell and the Buyer agrees to purchase 1000 units of advanced POS terminals, Model TV-2000, as per specifications attached in Schedule A.

2. PRICE AND PAYMENT TERMS
Total consideration: INR 2,50,00,000/-
Payment Schedule:
- 25% advance on signing: INR 62,50,000/-
- 25% on delivery: INR 62,50,000/-
- 50% within 30 days of delivery: INR 1,25,00,000/-

3. DELIVERY SCHEDULE
Delivery to be completed within 60 days from the date of signing this agreement, i.e., on or before 15th March, 2025.

4. WARRANTIES
The Seller warrants that all equipment shall be free from defects and shall perform as per specifications for a minimum period of 24 months from the date of delivery.

5. DEFAULT CLAUSE
In case of default by either party, the defaulting party shall pay liquidated damages equal to 10% of the total contract value.

6. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of India and subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Bangalore.

WITNESSES:
For M/s. TechVision Solutions Pvt. Ltd.
_________________________
Director

For M/s. Global Retail Chain Ltd.
_________________________
Director
```

## BREACH INCIDENT
- Delivery delayed by 45 days (actual delivery: 30th April, 2025)
- 15% of delivered units found defective
- Buyer withheld final payment of INR 1,25,00,000/-
- Seller claims force majeure due to supply chain disruptions

## LEGAL ISSUES TO ANALYZE
1. Whether delay constitutes breach of contract?
2. Entitlement to liquidated damages?
3. Can buyer legally withhold payment?
4. Applicability of force majeure clause?
5. Remedies available to both parties?
