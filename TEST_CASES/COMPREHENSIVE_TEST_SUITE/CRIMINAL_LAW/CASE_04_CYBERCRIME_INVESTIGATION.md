Police Station: Cyber Crime, Mumbai

Complainant: M/s. SecureBank Ltd.
Address: 100, Banking Street, Mumbai - 400001

OFFENCE: Under Sections 66, 66C, 66D of Information Technology Act, 2000
         Sections 420, 468, 471 of Indian Penal Code, 1860

FACTS OF THE CASE:

1. Between 1st October, 2024 and 10th November, 2024, unauthorized persons
   gained access to the banking systems of SecureBank Ltd.

2. The perpetrators created fake customer accounts and transferred
   funds totaling INR 8,50,00,000/- to various accounts across India.

3. Investigation revealed that the attack originated from multiple
   IP addresses, with primary traces pointing to servers outside India.

4. The attackers used sophisticated phishing techniques to obtain
   employee credentials and bypass security measures.

5. Digital forensic analysis identified the following evidence:
   - Malicious software installed on 12 workstations
   - Unauthorized access logs from 25th October, 2024
   - Money trail through 47 different bank accounts
   - Cryptocurrency conversions totaling INR 2,50,00,000/-

EVIDENCE COLLECTED:
1. Server logs and access records
2. Network traffic analysis
3. Malware samples from infected systems
4. Bank transaction records
5. IP address mapping and geolocation data
6. Employee interview transcripts

INVESTIGATION STATUS:
Ongoing - International cooperation requested through Interpol.
