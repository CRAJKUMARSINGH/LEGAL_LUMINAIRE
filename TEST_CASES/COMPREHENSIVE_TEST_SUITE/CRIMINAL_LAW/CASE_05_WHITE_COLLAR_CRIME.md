CHARGE SHEET

Court: Special Court for Economic Offences, Delhi
Case No. CBI/ECO/2024/567
Date: 20th December, 2024

ACCUSED:
1. Mr. A. Sharma - CEO, FinanceCorp Ltd.
2. Ms. P. Patel - CFO, FinanceCorp Ltd.
3. Mr. R. Verma - Auditor, Verma & Associates

OFFENCES:
- Section 420 IPC - Cheating
- Section 477A IPC - Falsification of accounts
- Section 13 Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988
- Sections of Companies Act, 2013

FACTS OF THE CASE:

1. FinanceCorp Ltd., a listed company, falsified financial statements
   for the financial years 2021-22, 2022-23, and 2023-24.

2. The company showed inflated revenues of INR 500 crores over three years
   through circular trading and fake invoices.

3. Investigation revealed:
   - 127 fake invoices totaling INR 250 crores
   - 45 shell companies used for money laundering
   - Bank accounts in tax havens with INR 150 crores
   - Undisclosed related party transactions of INR 75 crores

4. The accused persons personally benefited:
   - Mr. Sharma: INR 25 crores through bonus and stock options
   - Ms. Patel: INR 15 crores through fraudulent reimbursements
   - Mr. Verma: INR 5 crores as audit fees for concealment

5. SEBI investigation confirmed market manipulation
   and insider trading activities.

EVIDENCE:
1. Bank statements and transaction records
2. Email communications between accused
3. Fake invoices and supporting documents
4. Auditor reports and working papers
5. SEBI investigation reports
6. Witness statements from employees

PROSECUTION WITNESSES:
1. Whistleblower employee (Anonymous)
2. Bank officials
3. SEBI investigation team
4. Forensic accountants
5. Company employees (protected witnesses)

Special Public Prosecutor
