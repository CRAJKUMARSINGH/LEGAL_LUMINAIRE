#### **2. INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE**

**PURPOSE**: This template is designed to maintain a comprehensive chronological record of all events, filings, and proceedings in a legal case.

**USAGE GUIDELINES**:
1. Enter events in chronological order (oldest to newest)
2. Include all significant events, even minor ones
3. Be specific with dates and times
4. Include supporting document references
5. Update regularly as case progresses

**CATEGORIES**:
- **Pre-Litigation**: Events before filing
- **Litigation**: Court proceedings
- **Evidence**: Evidence-related events
- **Correspondence**: Communication events
- **Settlement**: Settlement-related events
- **Other**: Miscellaneous events

### **MAINTENANCE GUIDELINES**

#### **REGULAR UPDATES**
- **Frequency**: Update after every significant event
- **Responsibility**: Assigned to case manager
- **Review**: Weekly review by senior attorney
- **Verification**: Monthly verification of accuracy

#### **QUALITY CONTROL**
- **Completeness**: Ensure all events are recorded
- **Accuracy**: Verify all dates and details
- **Consistency**: Maintain consistent formatting
- **Clarity**: Ensure clear and concise descriptions

#### **ARCHIVAL**
- **Retention**: Keep chronology for case duration + 7 years
- **Storage**: Secure storage with proper backup
- **Access**: Controlled access to authorized personnel
- **Destruction**: Proper destruction after retention period
