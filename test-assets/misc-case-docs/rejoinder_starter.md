### **SAMPLE COMPLETED SECTION**

#### **SAMPLE RESPONSE TO OPPOSITION ARGUMENT**

**RESPONSE TO OPPOSITION ARGUMENT 1**: The contract is void due to lack of consideration

**OPPOSITION'S POSITION**:
The opposition argues that the contract dated January 15, 2024 is void for lack of consideration under Section 10 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

**OUR RESPONSE**:
We respectfully submit that the opposition's argument is misconceived and contrary to established legal principles. The contract in question is fully supported by adequate consideration as evidenced by:

1. Mutual exchange of promises between parties
2. Consideration of INR 50,00,000/- as evidenced in Clause 3
3. Performance of obligations by both parties
4. Consideration is lawful and sufficient

**LEGAL BASIS**:
- Section 2(d) of the Indian Contract Act, 1872 defines consideration
- Section 10 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872 requires consideration for valid contract
- Section 25 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872 provides exceptions to consideration requirement

**FACTUAL BASIS**:
- Contract clearly specifies consideration in Clause 3
- Consideration is evidenced by Annexure A - Bank Transfer Receipt
- Both parties have performed their respective obligations
- No evidence of lack of consideration

**JURISPRUDENTIAL SUPPORT**:
- M/s. ABC vs. XYZ (2019) 5 SCC 456 - Consideration must be lawful and sufficient
- M/s. PQR vs. LMN (2020) 8 SCC 789 - Performance constitutes consideration
- M/s. DEF vs. GHI (2021) 3 SCC 234 - Presumption of consideration in commercial contracts

**CONCLUSION**:
The opposition's argument that the contract is void for lack of consideration is without merit and contrary to established legal principles and evidence on record.

### **REJOINDER WRITING GUIDELINES**

#### **STRUCTURE**:
- Address each opposition argument separately
- Use clear headings and subheadings
- Maintain logical flow
- Include table of contents for longer rejoinders

#### **LANGUAGE**:
- Use respectful but firm language
- Avoid personal attacks
- Be precise and concise
- Use legal terminology correctly

#### **RESPONSE STRATEGY**:
- Address all opposition arguments
- Provide point-by-point responses
- Support responses with authority
- Clarify any misinterpretations

#### **CONTENT**:
- Focus on rebutting opposition arguments
- Clarify any misunderstandings
- Reinforce your original position
- Avoid introducing new arguments (unless necessary)

#### **COMPLIANCE**:
- Follow court rules and procedures
- Adhere to page limits
- Include required annexures
- Proper verification and signing
