- **Impact**: Document may be deemed invalid or inadmissible

- **Timeline**: Immediate - within 24 hours

#### **CRITICAL DEFICIENCIES**:
- Threaten document validity
- May result in legal consequences

- May delay proceedings

#### **MAJOR DEFICIENCIES**:
- Significantly impact document effectiveness
- May result in legal challenges
- Require urgent attention
- May affect case outcome

- Minor impact on document effectiveness
- Unlikely to result in legal consequences
- Require timely attention
- Affect document quality
