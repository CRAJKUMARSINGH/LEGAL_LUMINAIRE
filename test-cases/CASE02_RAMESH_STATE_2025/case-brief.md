## Rajasthan High Court

**FIR:** 234/2024 dated 15-03-2024, PS Sanganer, Jaipur
**Accused:** Ramesh Kumar Sharma, S/o Mohan Lal, Jaipur
**Charges:** NDPS Act §8/21/29 — possession of 2.5 kg heroin
**Court:** Rajasthan High Court, Jaipur Bench
**Case No.:** S.B. Criminal Misc. Bail Application No. 4521/2024

## Facts
Accused arrested at Sanganer bypass. Police claim 2.5 kg heroin found in vehicle boot.
Accused denies ownership. Vehicle belongs to third party. No independent witness.
Sampling procedure under NDPS Act §52A not followed — no Magistrate present.
No certificate under §52A(4) obtained. Samples not sent to FSL within prescribed time.

## Defence Strategy
1. Non-compliance with §52A NDPS — mandatory procedure violated
2. No independent witness — only police witnesses
3. Delay in FSL report — 45 days (prescribed: 30 days)
4. Vehicle not owned by accused — no nexus established
5. State of Punjab v. Baldev Singh (1999) 6 SCC 172 — §50 compliance mandatory
6. Mukesh v. State of Rajasthan (2019) — bail in NDPS where procedure violated
