# CASE_06 — Writ Petition — Land Acquisition
## Rajasthan High Court

**Writ:** W.P. (C) No. 8821/2024, HC Rajasthan, Jaipur
**Petitioner:** Ramji Lal Meena + 12 others, Farmers, Tonk District
**Respondent:** State of Rajasthan + RIICO
**Relief:** Quashing of land acquisition notification + enhanced compensation

## Facts
Land acquired under RFCTLARR Act 2013 for industrial zone.
Notification issued 15-01-2024. Social Impact Assessment (SIA) not conducted.
Compensation offered: Rs. 8 lakh/bigha. Market value: Rs. 25 lakh/bigha.
Consent of 80% affected families not obtained (mandatory for private company).
Urgency clause invoked — no justification provided.

## Defence Strategy
1. SIA not conducted — mandatory under RFCTLARR §4
2. Consent of 80% families not obtained — §2(2) violation
3. Urgency clause invoked without justification — §40 violation
4. Compensation grossly inadequate — market value not considered
5. Pune Municipal Corporation v. Harakchand (2014) 3 SCC 183 — SIA mandatory
6. Indore Development Authority v. Manoharlal (2020) 8 SCC 129 — compensation

## Expected Output
- Writ petition u/a 226 Constitution
- Grounds: SIA violation, consent violation, urgency clause misuse, inadequate compensation
- Precedents: Pune Municipal (2014), Indore Development (2020)
- Prayer: Quash notification + enhanced compensation
- Verification report
