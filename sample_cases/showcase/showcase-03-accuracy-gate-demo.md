  1. XYZ v. State of ABC (2025) 99 SCC 999 — FABRICATED
  2. Imaginary Judge v. Fictional State AIR 2024 SC 9999 — FABRICATED
  3. Sanjay Chandra v. CBI (2012) 1 SCC 40 — REAL

Step 1 — Accuracy Gate Runs
  Citation 1: XYZ v. State of ABC → NOT FOUND on Manupatra/SCC/Kanoon
    → Status: PENDING → blockedFromDraft: true
  Citation 2: Imaginary Judge v. Fictional State → NOT FOUND
    → Status: PENDING → blockedFromDraft: true
  Citation 3: Sanjay Chandra v. CBI (2012) → FOUND on Indian Kanoon
    → Status: SECONDARY → included with qualification

Step 2 — Warning Shown
  "⚠️ 2 citations could not be verified and have been blocked from your draft.
   Please verify these citations before filing:
   - XYZ v. State of ABC (2025) 99 SCC 999
   - Imaginary Judge v. Fictional State AIR 2024 SC 9999"

Step 3 — Draft Generated
  → Only Sanjay Chandra v. CBI included
  → Marked as SECONDARY with qualification note
  → Fabricated citations NOT in draft

Step 4 — Verification Report
  BLOCKED (PENDING): 2 citations
  SECONDARY: 1 citation
  VERIFIED: 0 citations
