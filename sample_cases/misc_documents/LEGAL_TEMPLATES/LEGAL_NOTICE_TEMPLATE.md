# LEGAL NOTICE TEMPLATE

## STANDARD LEGAL NOTICE FORMAT

### NOTICE HEADER
```
LEGAL NOTICE

Under: [Applicable Law/Act]
Section: [Relevant Section]
Ref: [Reference Number]
Date: [Date of Issue]

From:
[Advocate/Law Firm Name]
[Address]
[Phone Number]
[Email ID]
[Bar Council Enrollment No.]

To:
[Recipient Name]
[Recipient Address]
[Phone Number]
[Email ID]

Subject: LEGAL NOTICE REGARDING [Subject Matter]

Dear Sir/Madam,
```

### INTRODUCTION
```
Under the instructions and on behalf of my client [Client Name], residing at [Client Address], I hereby serve you with the following legal notice:

1. That my client is the [description of client's position/rights/title].

2. That you are [description of recipient's position/relationship to client].

3. That [brief description of background facts leading to dispute].
```

### FACTUAL MATRIX
```
4. That the relevant facts of the case are as follows:

   a. [Fact 1 with date and details]
   b. [Fact 2 with date and details]
   c. [Fact 3 with date and details]
   d. [Fact 4 with date and details]

5. That despite my client's repeated requests and reminders, you have failed to [describe failure/breach].

6. That your actions/inactions constitute a breach of [relevant legal provision/agreement term].

7. That due to your [breach/default], my client has suffered [description of damages/losses].
```

### LEGAL GROUNDS
```
8. That your actions are contrary to the provisions of:
   a. [Relevant Act/Statute], Section [section number]
   b. [Relevant Act/Statute], Section [section number]
   c. [Relevant case law: Name of case, citation]
   d. [Terms of contract/agreement]

9. That my client is entitled to the following reliefs under law:
   a. [Relief 1 with legal basis]
   b. [Relief 2 with legal basis]
   c. [Relief 3 with legal basis]
```

### DEMANDS/CLAIMS
```
10. That you are hereby called upon to:

    a. [Specific demand 1 with deadline]
    b. [Specific demand 2 with deadline]
    c. [Specific demand 3 with deadline]
    d. Payment of damages: INR [amount] within [time period]

11. That in case of your failure to comply with the above demands within [time period] from the receipt of this notice, my client shall be constrained to initiate appropriate civil/criminal proceedings against you at your sole risk, cost, and consequences.

12. That you shall also be liable for all costs and consequences including but not limited to:
    a. Legal fees and court costs
    b. Interest on damages @ [rate]% per annum
    c. Any other reliefs as deemed fit by the court
```

### CONCLUSION
```
13. A copy of this notice is retained in my office for further action.

14. This notice is issued without prejudice to any other legal rights and remedies available to my client.

15. Please treat this notice as most urgent and give your reply within the stipulated time.

Yours faithfully,

_________________________
[Advocate Name]
[Law Firm Name]
[Bar Council Enrollment No.]
[Contact Details]
```

### ACKNOWLEDGEMENT RECEIPT
```
ACKNOWLEDGEMENT OF RECEIPT

Received the above legal notice on: [Date and Time]
Receiver's Name: _________________________
Receiver's Signature: ______________________
Contact Number: ___________________________
```

## TYPES OF LEGAL NOTICES

### 1. BREACH OF CONTRACT NOTICE
- Specific clauses breached
- Performance requirements
- Damage calculations
- Cure period

### 2. PROPERTY DISPUTE NOTICE
- Title details
- Possession rights
- Encroachment details
- Relief claimed

### 3. EMPLOYMENT TERMINATION NOTICE
- Service details
- Grounds for termination
- Dues and benefits
- Compliance with labor laws

### 4. CHEQUE BOUNCE NOTICE
- Cheque details
- Bank return memo
- Demand under Section 138 NI Act
- 15-day notice period

### 5. RECOVERY NOTICE
- Outstanding amount
- Invoice details
- Payment terms
- Interest calculation

## DRAFTING GUIDELINES

### ESSENTIAL ELEMENTS:
1. Clear identification of parties
2. Detailed factual background
3. Specific legal grounds
4. Precise demands/claims
5. Reasonable time for compliance
6. Warning of consequences

### LEGAL REQUIREMENTS:
1. Proper service of notice
2. Compliance with limitation periods
3. Inclusion of all material facts
4. Proper legal citations
5. Professional tone and language

### BEST PRACTICES:
1. Verify all facts before sending
2. Keep copies of all communications
3. Use registered post/courier with acknowledgment
4. Maintain professional tone
5. Follow up within specified time
