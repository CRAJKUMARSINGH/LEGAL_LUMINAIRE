# COURT PETITION TEMPLATE

## STANDARD COURT PETITION FORMAT

### PETITION HEADER
```
IN THE [COURT NAME]
[COURT LOCATION]

[Court Type] PETITION NO. [NUMBER] OF [YEAR]
[CASE TYPE]

PETITIONER(S):
[Petitioner Name(s)]
Age: [Age]
Occupation: [Occupation]
Address: [Complete Address]

VERSUS

RESPONDENT(S):
[Respondent Name(s)]
Age: [Age]
Occupation: [Occupation]
Address: [Complete Address]

PETITION UNDER [APPLICABLE LAW/SECTION]
DATE OF FILING: [DATE]
VALUATION: [AMOUNT]
COURT FEE PAID: [AMOUNT]
```

### PRELIMINARY STATEMENTS
```
The petition of [Petitioner Name], residing at [address], is as follows:

1. That the petitioner is a [description of petitioner's status/capacity].

2. That the respondent is [description of respondent's status/capacity].

3. That this petition is filed under the provisions of [relevant law/section] for the following reliefs:

   a. [Relief 1]
   b. [Relief 2]
   c. [Relief 3]
   d. Any other reliefs as this Hon'ble Court deems fit

4. That this Hon'ble Court has jurisdiction to try this petition as:
   a. [Cause of action arose within jurisdiction]
   b. [Respondent resides within jurisdiction]
   c. [Subject matter falls within jurisdiction]

5. That the valuation of this petition is INR [amount] and court fee of INR [amount] has been duly paid.
```

### FACTUAL MATRIX
```
6. That the facts of the case are as follows:

   a. [Fact 1 with date, time, and relevant details]
   b. [Fact 2 with date, time, and relevant details]
   c. [Fact 3 with date, time, and relevant details]
   d. [Fact 4 with date, time, and relevant details]

7. That [description of relationship between parties].

8. That [description of events leading to dispute].

9. That [description of cause of action and when it arose].

10. That [description of attempts at resolution, if any].

11. That [description of current status and urgency].
```

### LEGAL GROUNDS
```
12. That the petitioner is entitled to the reliefs claimed on the following grounds:

    a. LEGAL GROUND 1:
       [Detailed legal ground with statutory provisions]
       [Relevant case law: Name of case, citation]
       [Explanation of how ground applies to facts]

    b. LEGAL GROUND 2:
       [Detailed legal ground with statutory provisions]
       [Relevant case law: Name of case, citation]
       [Explanation of how ground applies to facts]

    c. LEGAL GROUND 3:
       [Detailed legal ground with statutory provisions]
       [Relevant case law: Name of case, citation]
       [Explanation of how ground applies to facts]

13. That the respondent's actions/inactions are contrary to:
    a. [Statute/Act], Section [section number]
    b. [Statute/Act], Section [section number]
    c. [Constitutional provisions, if applicable]
    d. [Judicial precedents]
```

### JURISDICTION AND MAINTAINABILITY
```
14. That this Hon'ble Court has jurisdiction to entertain this petition because:

    a. TERRITORIAL JURISDICTION:
       [Explanation of territorial jurisdiction]

    b. PECUNIARY JURISDICTION:
       [Explanation of pecuniary jurisdiction]

    c. SUBJECT MATTER JURISDICTION:
       [Explanation of subject matter jurisdiction]

15. That this petition is maintainable because:
    a. [Legal requirement 1 satisfied]
    b. [Legal requirement 2 satisfied]
    c. [Legal requirement 3 satisfied]

16. That there is no alternative remedy available or the alternative remedy is not adequate.
```

### PRAYER FOR RELIEF
```
PRAYER

In view of the facts and circumstances stated above, the petitioner most respectfully prays that this Hon'ble Court may be pleased to:

a. Grant [specific relief 1];
b. Grant [specific relief 2];
c. Grant [specific relief 3];
d. Pass any other order/direction as this Hon'ble Court deems fit and proper;
e. Grant costs of this petition;

AND FOR THIS ACT OF KINDNESS, THE PETITIONER SHALL EVER PRAY.

PETITIONER
_________________________
[Petitioner Name]
[Signature]

THROUGH:
_________________________
[Advocate Name]
[Bar Council Enrollment No.]
[Address]
[Phone Number]
[Email ID]
```

### VERIFICATION
```
VERIFICATION

Verified at [Place] on this [Date] that the contents of this petition are true and correct to the best of my knowledge and belief. No part of it is false and nothing material has been concealed therefrom.

PETITIONER
_________________________
[Petitioner Name]
```

## TYPES OF COURT PETITIONS

### 1. CIVIL SUIT PETITION
- Title suit
- Injunction suit
- Recovery suit
- Specific performance suit

### 2. CRIMINAL PETITION
- Bail application
- Quashing petition
- Revision petition
- Criminal appeal

### 3. WRIT PETITION
- Habeas corpus
- Mandamus
- Prohibition
- Certiorari
- Quo warranto

### 4. FAMILY COURT PETITION
- Divorce petition
- Child custody
- Maintenance
- Adoption

### 5. COMPANY PETITION
- Oppression and mismanagement
- Winding up
- Shareholder disputes
- Director disqualification

## DRAFTING GUIDELINES

### ESSENTIAL ELEMENTS:
1. Proper court jurisdiction
2. Complete party details
3. Clear statement of facts
4. Specific legal grounds
5. Precise prayer for relief
6. Proper verification

### LEGAL REQUIREMENTS:
1. Limitation period compliance
2. Court fee payment
3. Proper service of notice
4. Attachment of documents
5. Signature requirements

### BEST PRACTICES:
1. Chronological order of facts
2. Legal citations with case law
3. Clear and concise language
4. Proper formatting
5. Professional presentation
