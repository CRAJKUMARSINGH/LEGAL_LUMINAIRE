FINDINGS:

1. CLAIM NO. 1 (Site Handover Delay — ₹4.82 Cr): ALLOWED IN FULL.
   The Employer failed to hand over site within 15 days as required by GCC Clause 5. The 45-day delay is established by documentary evidence. The Claimant is entitled to full compensation.

2. CLAIM NO. 2 (Variation & Extra Items — ₹6.15 Cr): ALLOWED IN FULL.
   All three Variation Orders were issued in writing by the Engineer-in-Charge. Joint measurements confirm execution. The Claimant is entitled to payment.

3. CLAIM NO. 3 (Price Escalation — ₹3.94 Cr): ALLOWED IN FULL.
   CPWD Clause 10CC entitles the Claimant to price escalation. WPI indices are verified from official sources.

4. CLAIM NO. 4 (Idle Machinery & Labour — ₹2.18 Cr): ALLOWED IN PART (₹1.37 Cr).
   Log books and muster rolls support the claim partially. Expert certification not obtained for full amount.

5. TERMINATION: DECLARED ILLEGAL.
   The termination notice dated 15.01.2026 is set aside. Two valid EOTs were in force. The Employer cannot terminate for delay caused by its own defaults.

6. COUNTER CLAIMS: DISMISSED.
   All counter claims are dismissed as the delay was caused by the Employer.

---

AWARD:

The Claimant is awarded:
- Principal Amount: ₹16.28 Crore (Claims 1+2+3+4 partial)
- Interest @ 9% p.a. from 10.02.2026 till date of award: ₹1.46 Cr
- Post-award interest @ 9% p.a. till realization
- Costs of arbitration: ₹12 Lakh (to be borne by Respondent)

TOTAL AWARD: ₹17.86 Crore + post-award interest

The Performance Bank Guarantee (₹2.43 Cr) shall be released to the Claimant within 30 days.
