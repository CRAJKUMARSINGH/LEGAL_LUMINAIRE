CLAIM NO. 1 — DELAY IN SUPPLY OF 220 kV GIS EQUIPMENT
Amount: ₹9.85 Crore | Status: VERIFIED (95%)

FACTS:
- As per Special Conditions of Contract (Clause 12), RVPNL was responsible for procurement and supply of 220 kV GIS modules.
- Contractor mobilised resources immediately after site handover on 25.05.2024.
- GIS equipment scheduled to arrive by October 2024 — actually delivered March 2025 (5 months late).
- Cascading delay in civil foundation, erection, and testing works.

CLAUSE: FIDIC Sub-Clause 2.1 + Special Conditions Clause 12 (Employer Supplied Materials)

CALCULATION:
- Establishment & overhead: ₹72 Lakh/month × 5 months = ₹3.60 Cr
- Idle tower erection machinery (2 sets): ₹95 Lakh/month × 5 months = ₹4.75 Cr
- Idle skilled manpower (45 persons): ₹1.20 Cr
- Loss of productivity: ₹0.30 Cr
TOTAL CLAIM 1: ₹9.85 Crore

EVIDENCE: Work Order + Special Conditions, 42 emails (15.07.2024 to 20.02.2025), Delivery challans + delay certificates, Photographs of idle machinery, Monthly Progress Reports.

VERIFIED PRECEDENTS:
- NTPC Ltd. v. M/s. Siemens (Supreme Court, 2022) — Employer liable for delay in supply of employer-furnished equipment.
- Power Grid Corporation cases on employer-supplied material delay.

CLAIM NO. 5 — LOSS OF PROFIT & HEAD OFFICE OVERHEAD
Amount: ₹3.80 Crore | Status: SECONDARY

FORMULA APPLIED: Hudson Formula
Contract Profit Margin: 8% (as per tender)
Value of Unexecuted Work: ₹47.50 Crore (52% progress → 48% unexecuted × ₹68.25 Cr × 100%)
Delay Period: 5 months (GIS delay — primary cause)

Calculation:
= 8% × ₹47.50 Cr × (5/21)
= 8% × ₹47.50 Cr × 0.238

+ Loss of opportunity on other contracts: ₹2.33 Cr
