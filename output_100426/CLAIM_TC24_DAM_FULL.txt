CLAIM NO. 1 — UNFORESEEN GEOLOGICAL CONDITIONS
Amount: ₹18.75 Crore | Status: VERIFIED (97%)

FACTS:
- Foundation level (Chainage 0+000 to 0+450) encountered massive hard rock (Grade V) and swelling clay (Montmorillonite) not indicated in tender bore logs.
- Original bore logs (4 boreholes) showed soft alluvium to 8m depth.
- Post-discovery bore logs (12 boreholes) confirmed hard rock at 2-4m depth and swelling clay at 6-10m depth.
- IIT Jodhpur expert report confirms conditions were unforeseeable.
- Contractor required to blast rock (not in original scope) and install special foundation treatment.

CLAUSE: FIDIC Sub-Clause 4.12 (Unforeseeable Physical Conditions)

CALCULATION:
- Rock blasting (12,500 cum @ ₹850/cum): ₹1.06 Cr
- Special foundation treatment (grouting + anchoring): ₹4.85 Cr
- Revised foundation design (WRD's instruction): ₹6.40 Cr
- Delay compensation (4 months × ₹1.61 Cr/month): ₹6.44 Cr
TOTAL CLAIM 1: ₹18.75 Crore

EVIDENCE: Original bore logs, Post-discovery bore logs, IIT Jodhpur expert report, WRD's revised foundation design, Blasting records, Photographs.

CLAIM NO. 3 — DELAY IN APPROVAL OF MATERIALS
Amount: ₹7.85 Crore | Status: VERIFIED (85%)

FACTS:
- Cement and steel approvals delayed by 3-5 months.
- Contractor's proposed quarry sources (Rajsamand Quarry, Nathdwara Quarry) rejected twice without valid technical reason.
- WRD's approval committee met only once in 3 months — administrative delay.
- Caused disruption to concrete pouring schedule for dam body (Blocks 1-8).
- Concrete pour for Block 3 delayed by 4 months — critical path impact.

CLAUSE RELIED UPON:
- CPWD GCC 2020 Cl. 11 — Material approvals within 30 days of submission
- FIDIC Sub-Clause 7.1 — Employer to approve materials within reasonable time

DETAILED CALCULATION:
(a) Overhead during material approval delay (4 months): ₹1.85 Cr
(b) Idle concrete batching plants (3 units × ₹45 Lakh/month × 4 months): ₹5.40 Cr
(c) Idle skilled concrete workers (38 persons × ₹750/day × 120 days): ₹0.60 Cr
TOTAL CLAIM 3: ₹7.85 Crore

EVIDENCE:
1. Material approval submissions (3 submissions — all on record)
2. WRD rejection letters (2 rejections without technical basis)
3. WRD approval committee meeting minutes (showing 3-month gap)
4. Concrete pour schedule vs. actual (critical path analysis)
5. Idle plant log books
6. Labour muster rolls
