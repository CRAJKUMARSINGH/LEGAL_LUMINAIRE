NHAI Project Implementation Unit - Udaipur
Standing Committee Meeting
Date: 12.02.2026

Agenda: Review of NH-758 45 km 2-Lane Expansion Project

Observations:
- The progress stands at a dismal 38% against the scheduled target of 85% at this stage.
- Contractor has repeatedly halted work citing non-payment of bills and site handover delays.
- Independent Engineer reports that the contractor has not mobilized sufficient machinery for the earthwork phase.

Decision on NH-758 Project:
- The Committee recommends termination of the contract under Clause 62 of GCC.
- Recommended blacklisting of M/s. Rajputana Infra Projects Pvt. Ltd. for 3 years.
- Forfeiture of Performance Bank Guarantee of ₹5.63 Cr.
