Water Resources Department Standing Committee
Date: 10.02.2026

Agenda: Construction of Banas Dam
Contractor: M/s. Rajasthan Construction Consortium

Findings:
- Progress has been severely lacking; only 29% physical progress against 50% target.
- Contractor's claim of geological surprises (hard rock excavation) considered but rejected as it is within normal execution variances. Tenders strictly advise contractors to assess site conditions (GCC Clause 11).
- The contractor failed to mobilize adequate drilling machines. 

Decision:
- Recommend termination of the contract under Clause 3 of GCC due to slow progress.
- Encashment of the Bank Guarantee.
