**Contract Value:** ₹ 68,20,00,000 (Sixty-Eight Crore Twenty Lakh)

**Completion Period:** 36 months (by 09.07.2022)  
**Arbitration Status:** Award passed 30.08.2023 — ₹ 18,43,00,000 to Contractor

## Key Legal Issues

1. **Geological surprises** — risk allocation under GCC Clause 12: "Physical Conditions more adverse than foreseeable"  
2. **Force Majeure** dual events (COVID + monsoon) — CPWD GCC Clause 65 + NBC 2016 s.3.4  
3. **Design revision** — Employer-caused variation vs. contractor's adaptation obligation  
4. **LD set-off** — delay entirely Employer's fault; LD clause cannot operate: Alstom v. Board of Trustees, Visakhapatnam Port (2004) — arbitrator's finding binding
